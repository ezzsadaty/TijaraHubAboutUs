Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to common questions about our services, features, and how TijaraHub can help your business grow.

Becoming a seller on Tijarahub involves the following steps:
Sign Up: Register for a seller account by providing necessary business details and contact information.
Verification: Submit required documentation for verification, including business licenses and tax information.
Setup: Complete your seller profile, including uploading product listings, setting prices, and defining shipping options.
Training: Participate in onboarding sessions and review training materials to familiarize yourself with the platform.
Launch: Once approved, your products will go live, and you can start receiving orders.

Tijarahub offers flexible logistics and shipping solutions to streamline the process:
You can choose from various shipping partners integrated into the platform for efficient delivery such as FedEx, UPS, and DHL.

Sellers can monitor their performance through:
Dashboard Analytics: Access a comprehensive dashboard with real-time sales data, including total sales, order volume, and revenue.

TijaraHub prioritizes the security of transactions on its platform. We employ robust security measures and encryption protocols and regularly update our systems to safeguard user data and ensure secure business operations.

TijaraHub serves as a facilitator for transactions between buyers and suppliers. In case of any issues with products or services, please refer to the terms and conditions outlined in the Agreement with Supplier. TijaraHub will support the exchange of information between parties and assist in dispute resolution as needed.
